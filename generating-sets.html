Generating Sets

We procure, supply and install generator sets for residential, commercial, and industrial operations. Whether you need standby power, prime power, or a dedicated backup solution, we help you select the right capacity and configuration, then support delivery, installation, and commissioning.

Our approach is practical: we assess load demand, usage pattern, site conditions, and fuel preference, then recommend options that balance performance, reliability, and lifecycle cost.

What’s Included

End-to-end support from specification to commissioning.

  • Load assessment and capacity recommendation

  • Procurement and supply of generator sets

  • Installation coordination and commissioning support

  • Operational guidance and maintenance planning

How do I know the right generator size?

We size it based on your total load (running and starting), usage pattern, and growth plan. Share your equipment list or site details and we’ll recommend the appropriate capacity.

Do you handle supply and installation?

Yes. We procure and supply generator sets and can support installation and commissioning, depending on your project scope and site requirements.

Can you support after-sales maintenance?

Yes. We provide maintenance planning, routine servicing, and major repairs to keep your equipment reliable. You can also source genuine spare parts through us.

Do you offer rentals?

Yes. If buying isn’t ideal for your timeline, we offer rental generator sets for events, projects, and temporary power needs.
