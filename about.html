About IOJ

Who we are

Reliable Power & Engineering Support for Critical Operations

IOJ Integrated Services Limited is a Nigerian engineering and technical services company. We procure, supply, and install generating sets and industrial machines, and we provide the spare parts, rentals, maintenance, repairs, and engineering support required to keep operations stable.

Our focus is simple: reduce downtime, protect assets, and help clients run reliably with the right equipment, the right parts, and the right technical team behind it.

  • Generator Supply, Installation & Rentals

    End-to-end support from selection to installation and commissioning, plus short and long-term rentals for projects and emergency power needs.

  • Spare Parts, Maintenance & Major Repairs

    Genuine parts sourcing, preventive maintenance, troubleshooting, overhauls, and major repairs to extend equipment life and reduce downtime.

  • Engineering Procurements & Consultancy

    Engineering and general procurements, vendor support, and professional consultancy to guide decisions and strengthen operational reliability.
