Technical Consultancy for Power and Industrial Systems

Our consultancy service is designed for clients who need clear, practical guidance before they buy, install, repair, or upgrade equipment. We help you make the right decisions quickly, using real-world operating conditions and budget constraints as the baseline.

Whether you are selecting the right generating set capacity, improving system reliability, reducing fuel consumption, or planning maintenance, we provide recommendations you can act on.

What We Help With

Common consultancy requests we handle.

  • Generator sizing and load assessment

  • Installation planning and site readiness checks

  • Fault diagnosis support and repair planning

  • Maintenance planning and reliability improvement

What should I share for a consultation?

Share your challenge, current equipment details (brand/model/kVA/kW), operating hours, fault history (if any), and photos of the setup if possible. The more context you provide, the more accurate the recommendation.

Do you also supply what you recommend?

Yes. If your consultation results in an item list or upgrade plan, we can procure, supply, and install where required.

Can you advise on spare parts compatibility?

Yes. We help confirm part compatibility based on model/serial information and recommend quality alternatives when OEM parts are unavailable.

Do you provide maintenance schedules?

Yes. We can recommend preventive maintenance schedules based on operating hours and site conditions, and also support the execution under our maintenance services.
